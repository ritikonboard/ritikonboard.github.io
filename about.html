Scribbles

Personal thoughts, reflections, and moments in broken words.

January 19, 2025

So it took me almost 3 months to come back here and write something. I came back with the thought of removing the below article but now I think that I should just keep it as it is because these thoughts are mine.

Bon Iver is playing in the background as I write this, I'll write this along with the album 'Bon Iver, Bon Iver'. Holocene has started.

I have this year to make it all happen, and this will be the defining year. I have held myself back for too long now, but not anymore. I had a discussion with my friend and we came up with an idea to write down 12 things that I will be doing this year as video titles. These will be some of the major things that I will be working on/doing this year, starting today.

The list:

  1. Get a job/research intern
  2. Moving to a new city
  3. Meeting new people and experiences
  4. Get in the best shape of my life
  5. I went to the mountains
  6. But first get a camera
  7. Make the best short film of my life
  8. Figure out where I see myself for the coming years in career/academic aspect
  9. Make good films on YouTube
  10. I went on roadtrip with a stranger (this one's crazy I don't even know why did I write this)
  11. Read Hindi literature, philosophy
  12. Spend time with the people that have my heart
October 11, 2024

I still don't know what to write here after keeping this section empty for almost 3 months now. Was just updating something else on my webpage and thought of writing something here today.

With only around 3 months remaining, I feel a sense of urgency and the need to do something significant for myself. It has been a tough time, and if I keep going like this then it will be worse. I can sense that.

There's a constant battle within between dreams and reality every single day. I believe my life lies in the intersection, and that I can't let any one of them go. And I know it is going to be hell tough.

Quoting Pash - "Sabse khatarnaak hota hai humare sapno ka mar jana."

I remember reading this in my Hindi textbook in school, and recently a few random events led me to come across these words again. I have no idea how I'll be doing it but I know one thing that I am not letting my dreams die.

More thoughts and reflections will appear here as I continue this journey...
